Jesus of Nazareth — Teacher, Savior, Light of the World

Jesus of Nazareth, also known as Jesus Christ, is the central figure of Christianity and one of the most influential individuals in history. He preached love, humility, forgiveness, and peace, and His life continues to inspire billions across the world.

His teachings such as "Love your neighbor as yourself" and "Blessed are the peacemakers" remain timeless calls for compassion and understanding.

Believed to be born around 4 BC in Bethlehem and crucified around AD 30–33, Jesus' legacy lives on through scripture, service, and the example He set through His life.
